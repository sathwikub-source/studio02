# **App Name**: StudySpot

## Core Features:

- Role-Based Authentication & Access: Secure user registration, login, and management with distinct Student, Lecturer, and Admin portals.
- Course & Enrollment Management: Admins and Lecturers can create, edit, and remove courses. Students can browse and enroll in available courses.
- Material & Assignment Management: Lecturers can upload various lecture materials (PDF, PPT, video) and create assignments. Students can view materials and submit assignments.
- Grading & Feedback System: Lecturers can grade submitted assignments and provide feedback. Students can view their grades and feedback within their portal.
- User-Specific Dashboards: Personalized dashboards for students, lecturers, and admins displaying relevant information such as enrolled courses, upcoming assignments, or platform overview.
- AI-Powered Document Summary Tool: A tool to generate concise summaries for uploaded lecture materials (e.g., PDFs, PPTs) to assist students in quickly grasping key concepts.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A deep, professional blue (#2231B8) conveying trust and knowledge, reminiscent of academic settings and providing strong contrast for text in a light scheme.
- Background color: A very light, desaturated blue (#ECEEF3) that promotes readability and a clean aesthetic, creating a serene environment for focus and learning.
- Accent color: A vibrant purple (#A33DCC) derived from the primary blue, offering a touch of creativity and modernity, ideal for highlighting interactive elements and calls to action.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for its modern, clean, and highly readable design, ensuring clarity for both headings and extensive academic content.
- Utilize simple, outline-style icons that complement the clean, academic theme, maintaining a consistent visual language across all portals.
- Implement a modular and responsive layout with clear delineation between content sections, ensuring optimal viewing and navigation across devices, consistent with a Google Classroom-like interface.
